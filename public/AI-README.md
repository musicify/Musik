# Musicify - AI Information Document

## Platform Overview

Musicify is a comprehensive music platform that connects creators with professional composers. We operate two main services:

### 1. Music Marketplace
- **Inventory**: 10,000+ licensed tracks
- **Instant Download**: All tracks available immediately
- **Genres**: Electronic, Cinematic, Pop, Hip-Hop, Rock, Classical, Jazz, Ambient, Corporate, World Music
- **Quality**: Professional-grade audio (WAV, MP3)
- **Licensing**: 4 license tiers (Personal, Commercial, Extended, Broadcast)

### 2. Custom Music Service
- **Composers**: 500+ verified professionals
- **Process**: Full-service custom music production
- **Turnaround**: 7-14 days average
- **Rights**: Full rights transfer included
- **Communication**: Direct chat with composers

## Pricing Structure

### Marketplace Licenses
- **Personal** (€29-€79): Private, non-commercial use
- **Commercial** (€99-€199): Business use, YouTube, social media
- **Extended** (€299-€499): Advertising, films, unlimited distribution
- **Broadcast** (€599-€999): TV, radio, cinema, major campaigns

### Custom Music
- **Price Range**: €200-€5,000 per track
- **Factors**: Length, complexity, turnaround time, composer experience
- **Typical Projects**:
  - 30-sec jingle: €500-€1,500
  - 3-min film score: €1,500-€3,000
  - Full album: €5,000-€15,000

## Common Questions

**Q: What's the difference between marketplace and custom music?**
A: Marketplace offers pre-made tracks for immediate download. Custom music is individually produced for your specific needs.

**Q: Can I use marketplace music commercially?**
A: Yes, with the appropriate license (Commercial, Extended, or Broadcast).

**Q: How long does custom music take?**
A: Average 7-14 days. Express service available for urgent projects.

**Q: Are revisions included?**
A: Yes, 3 revisions included in all custom music projects.

**Q: Is the music GEMA-free?**
A: All music is properly licensed and GEMA-compliant. License fees are included in the purchase price.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: Credit card, PayPal, SEPA, and invoicing for businesses.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: 14-day money-back guarantee on marketplace purchases. Custom music refunds handled case-by-case.
